Caawinaad Degdeg ah

Haddii aad dhibaato kala kulanto foomka boos qabsashada, fadlan nagala soo xiriir: support@somalisafari.com

1. Sideen u bixinayaa lacagta safarka?
Waxaad ku bixin kartaa adeegyada EVC Plus, Premier Wallet, ama kaarka Visa-ha.
2. Ma baajin karaa safarkeyga haddii ay xaaladi is badasho?
Haa, waxaad baajin kartaa 48 saac ka hor waqtiga safarka si laguu soo celiyo lacagtaada.
3. Ma bixisaan adeeg caawinaad fiiso (Visa)?
Haa, safarada caalamiga ah badankooda anaga ayaa fududayna habka codsiga fiisaha.
